HOLD YOUR EMPLOYEES ACCOUNTABLE
When operating a small business, it's important to be an effective manager of field technicians and staff. One of the difficulties of making field calls is ensuring that your employees honestly report their time. Having each dispatched employee using a smartphone with GPS technology enables Foxbuddy to track their whereabouts. This allows an accurate measurement of total time spent on each job or project. Not only does this hold your employees accountable for their work, but it ensures that your customers are billed accurately for the labor accumulated.

SAVE TIME
Using a mobile online calendar and booking system saves you time. Instead of consulting a master calendar or having to call customers back to schedule new appointments, you can drag and drop your work schedule on the go and include relevant information about your appointments. Instantaneously all other jobs are rescheduled and all staff and clients are notified with Push, SMS or Email Notifications.
Mobile devices also reduce the amount of time you spend on paperwork. In the past, all transactions had to be recorded on paper; you may have printed invoices in your home office and faxed or mailed them to customers. With a smartphone or tablet you have all this information already available with you. When your staff is required to report a job they can simply record videos, images, and audio, these actions that are faster to be executed especially on field. Photos and videos are more powerful and can include informations that often are left by in a written report. After completing a house call or establishing a contract, all informations are instantly accessible to admin staff. This avoids the hassle to report and the risk of forgetting or loosing informations.
